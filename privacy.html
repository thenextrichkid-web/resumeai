Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

Short version: We do not store your resume. We do not sell your data. Your resume text is sent to the AI for analysis and immediately discarded. We do not create accounts or track personal information without your consent.

1. Who we are

ResumeAI is a product of Alliance Business Services, an AI consulting agency based in India. We provide AI-powered resume analysis tools to help job seekers improve their chances of passing Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS).

Contact: alliancebusiness.abs@gmail.com

2. What data we collect

When you use ResumeAI, here is what happens with your data:

  • Resume text: Your resume content is sent to the AI model (Claude by Anthropic) for analysis. It is not stored on our servers after your session ends.
  • Job description text: If you paste a job description, it is similarly sent for analysis only and not stored.
  • Scan count: We store a simple counter in your browser's local storage (on your device) to track your free scan usage. This stays on your device only.
  • Payment information: If you upgrade to Pro via UPI or WhatsApp, payment is handled directly between you and the payment provider. We do not store card or bank details.

3. What data we do NOT collect

  • We do not create user accounts or store your email address unless you contact us
  • We do not store your resume on our servers
  • We do not share your resume with any third parties other than the AI provider for processing
  • We do not track your location or personal identity
  • We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels

4. Third-party AI processing

Your resume text is processed by Claude (made by Anthropic, USA) to generate your ATS score and suggestions. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to this processing. Anthropic does not use your data to train their models when accessed via API. You can review Anthropic's privacy policy at anthropic.com/privacy.

5. Cookies and local storage

We use browser local storage (not cookies) solely to remember your remaining free scan count. No personal data is stored. You can clear this at any time by clearing your browser data.

6. Children's privacy

ResumeAI is intended for adults and job seekers aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Your rights

Since we do not store your personal data, there is nothing to delete. If you have contacted us via email, you may request deletion of that correspondence at any time by emailing alliancebusiness.abs@gmail.com.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as we add new features. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the service after changes means you accept the updated policy.

9. Contact

For any privacy questions or concerns, email us at alliancebusiness.abs@gmail.com. We respond within 2 business days.
