Privacy Policy

Effective April 15, 2026
Executive Summary: We do not store your resumes. We do not sell your data. Your career information is processed in real-time by AI and then purged from our active memory. We only store your Pilot Name and Email locally on your device.

1. Data Acquisition & Processing

When you initiate a tactical scan, ResumeAI collects the following information for real-time analysis:

  • Resume Data: Extracted text from uploaded PDF or DOCX files.
  • Contextual Data: Target Job Descriptions provided for AI calibration.
  • Identity Data: Name and Email (stored strictly via LocalStorage on your device).

2. Third-Party Intelligence

To provide high-tier tactical reports, your data is securely routed via n8n and processed by Google Gemini. These sub-processors are bound by strict privacy agreements and do not use your personal career data to train global AI models.

3. Data Retention Protocol

We believe in "Zero-Persistence" security:

  • Scan Data: Discarded immediately after the report is generated.
  • Local Memory: Your name and email stay in your browser. Clearing your cache removes this data instantly.
  • Lead Logs: We maintain a record of your name, email, and score in our secure lead database (Google Sheets) for support and "Pro" upgrade verification.

4. Financial Security

Payments for "Tactical Reconstruct (PRO)" are handled manually via UPI or WhatsApp. ResumeAI never has access to your banking credentials, UPI PINs, or card details.
