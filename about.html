The story behind ResumeAI

ResumeAI was not built in a boardroom. It was built out of personal frustration.

Our founder Mr.Ajay Reddy, spent months applying to jobs and getting zero responses. Not because of a lack of skills or experience — but because of a 23/100 ATS score on a resume that was never optimised for automated screening. 75% of resumes never reach a human recruiter. They are rejected silently by software.

That frustration experience gave birth to this product. A tool that gives every job seeker — especially freshers and mid-level professionals in India — a fair shot at being seen. We built it with AI, launched it for free, and growing it one user at a time.

This is what we do at Alliance Business Services: we find real problems, build AI-powered solutions, and make them accessible.

"Our mission is to make AI accessible and practical for everyone in India — not just large corporations. Every job seeker, small business, and professional deserves to benefit from the power of artificial intelligence."
