Tactical Methodology

How our Proprietary Intelligence Engine reconstructs your professional narrative.

01. What is the 85% Rule?

Modern Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) use keyword density and formatting parsers to filter candidates. Our research shows that resumes scoring below 85% in "Narrative Alignment" are often automatically disqualified. We identify the specific gaps preventing you from hitting this threshold.

02. Deep Narrative Analysis

Our engine performs a binary-level scan of your document. It evaluates "Narrative Weight"—the ratio of impact-based verbs to passive descriptions—ensuring your resume reads like a tactical achievement record rather than a simple duty list.

03. Precision JD Calibration

Unlike generic scanners, we calibrate your resume against the specific job description provided. We analyze the semantic intent of the employer's requirements and cross-reference them with your career data to ensure a high-fidelity match.

04. Why is the second scan "Pro"?

The initial scan identifies vulnerabilities. The secondary Tactical Reconstruct involves deeper processing layers that offer specific, line-by-line reconstruction advice. This high-compute analysis is reserved for our Pro-tier Pilots.

05. Data Security & Purge

In accordance with the AXONIC S.R. privacy protocol, your resume data is processed in a temporary environment. We do not maintain permanent copies of your documents on our primary servers.
